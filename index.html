Kevin Barcenas

Hello!

My name is Kevin. I am a Mexican-American who grew up in central Texas. I graduated with a bachelor's in Computer Science and a bachelor's in Linguistics from the University of Texas at Austin. Throughout my undergrad, I dabbled a bit in everything. I initially started as just a CS major, took a couple of Mexican-American Studies classes, business, and religious studies. Until I arrived as an undergraduate researcher at the Linguistic Research Center. Language and linguistics as a whole became the humanities that contemplated my CS degree.

I am grateful to have been able to be part of many projects, side-hustles, part-time jobs, and research opportunities throughout my four years in Austin. It's shaped my outlook on life, ideologies, and more than anything I was able to make relationships I would not trade for anything in the world.

I'm currently working at Intuit as a software engineer.
